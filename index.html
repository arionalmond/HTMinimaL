Web Dev, Sep 18, 2024

Finally Learning React..

After reading through some initial documenation to refresh on use cases for React, I paused when I got to the installation section. Seeing that React suggests a framework to start a project, kind of threw me off. "Isn't this a framework?" I asked myself. "I need a framework for this framework?"

I do not have a lot of experience in UI/UX/Front End development, but then and there, I knew it may be better for me to refresh on the basics. Compare again, Angular vs. React and Next.js, Vue vs. Svelte. This site serves multiple purposes, for learning, comparing frameworks, and running into the problems myself before being told to use something before running into the problem. May be crazy but here we are.
